# Quick Start Guide - Pharmacy ERP System
## 5-Minute Quick Reference

### First Time Login
1. Open: `http://yourpharmacy.com:4000`
2. Enter email and password
3. Change password on first login
4. You're ready to go!

---

## Dashboard Quick Tour (30 seconds)

| Widget | What It Shows |
|--------|--------------|
| **Total Stock Value** | How much inventory worth (in rupees) |
| **Low Stock Items** | What needs to be reordered |
| **Expiring Soon** | Medicines expiring within 30 days |
| **Today's Sales** | Total money earned today |
| **Pending Approvals** | Items waiting for your yes/no |

---

## Processing Your First Sale (2 minutes)

```
Step 1: Click "POS" in the sidebar
Step 2: Scan medicine barcode OR search by name
Step 3: Enter quantity
Step 4: Repeat for all medicines
Step 5: Click "Complete Sale"
Step 6: Select payment method (Cash/Card/UPI)
Step 7: Click "Confirm"
Done! Bill prints automatically ✓
```

---

## Receiving Stock from Supplier (3 minutes)

```
Step 1: Click "Purchase Orders"
Step 2: Click "Receive Goods"
Step 3: Select PO to receive
Step 4: Enter batch number & expiry date for each item
Step 5: Upload supplier invoice
Step 6: Click "Complete Receipt"
Stock updated automatically ✓
```

---

## Checking Stock Levels (1 minute)

```
Click "Inventory" → "Stock Management"
See: Name | Quantity | Price | Reorder Level
Can filter by: Status | Category | Supplier
```

---

## Creating Purchase Order (2 minutes)

```
Step 1: Click "Purchase Orders" → "New Order"
Step 2: Select supplier
Step 3: Add medicines:
   - Click "Add Item"
   - Search medicine
   - Enter quantity needed
Step 4: Review total
Step 5: Click "Create"
Send to supplier via email/WhatsApp ✓
```

---

## What to Do When...

### 🚨 Medicine Expires Tomorrow
- Click "Inventory" → "Expiry Tracking"
- Find medicine
- Plan: Sell at discount OR return to supplier

### 📉 Stock Running Low
- System shows alert on dashboard
- Click alert → Creates PO automatically
- Fill details and send to supplier

### 🧮 Month-End Report
- Click "Reports" → Choose report type
- Select month
- Click "Export to PDF" or "Print"
- Share with accountant

### 💳 Need to Pay Supplier
- Click "Suppliers"
- Find supplier
- Click "Create Payment"
- Select invoices to pay
- Done!

### ❌ Wrong Stock Count?
- Click "Inventory" → "Adjustment"
- Select medicine
- Enter correct quantity
- Click "Submit"

## Common Fields Explained

**Medicine Code**: Unique ID (e.g., PARA500)
**Reorder Level**: When to order more (e.g., 10 units)
**Unit Price**: Cost per unit from supplier
**HSN Code**: For GST calculation (5 digits)
**Batch Number**: Manufacturer batch tracking
**Expiry Date**: Last day medicine is safe to sell
